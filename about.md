## About Damone

I grew up driven by a curiosity for how systems think and communicate — which led me to computer science. I started at Forsyth Technical Community College, earned my Associate's degree in 2024, and transferred to Winston-Salem State University to complete my Bachelor's. I graduate in 2027.

My work sits at the intersection of software engineering and artificial intelligence. I'm particularly drawn to graph theory, knowledge representation, and building tools that extract meaning from complex, connected data.
